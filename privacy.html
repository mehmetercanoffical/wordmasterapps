Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 21, 2026 • Last Updated: March 21, 2026

Welcome to WordMaster ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices when you use the WordMaster mobile application (the "App").

Important: By using WordMaster, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account and use WordMaster, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information:
    • Email address (required for account creation)
    • Display name (optional, user-chosen username)
    • Profile photo (optional, from Google Sign-In or uploaded)
    • Password (encrypted, never stored in plain text)
  • Profile Information:
    • Age range (e.g., 13-17, 18-25, 26-35, 36-50, 50+)
    • Gender (male, female, prefer not to say)
    • English proficiency level (beginner, elementary, intermediate, upper intermediate, advanced)
    • Native language and target learning language
    • Learning goals (travel, work, exam preparation, entertainment, daily life)
    • Daily study time preference (5, 10, 15, or 20 minutes)
    • Struggle areas (listening, speaking, vocabulary, grammar, writing)
  • Learning Progress Data:
    • Words learned and favorited
    • Test scores, correct/incorrect answer counts per word
    • Daily goals, streak days, longest streak
    • Total XP (experience points), current league (Unranked, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond)
    • Unlocked badges and achievements
    • Study session history and activity calendar
    • Skill scores (Reading, Listening, Grammar, Speaking)
    • Word-specific performance metrics (correct/incorrect counts, last tested date)
    • Multi-language learning progress (separate tracking per target language)
  • App Settings:
    • Language preferences for app interface
    • Notification preferences
    • Sound effects and text-to-speech settings
    • Home view mode (spiral or classic)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information:
    • Device model, manufacturer, and operating system version (iOS/Android)
    • Unique device identifiers (for crash reporting and analytics)
    • App version number
  • Usage Analytics:
    • Features used, test types completed (Visual Quiz, Spelling Bee, Listening Test, etc.)
    • Session duration and frequency
    • Navigation patterns within the App
  • Performance & Crash Data:
    • App crashes, errors, and performance issues (via Firebase Crashlytics)
    • Network connectivity status (to enable offline mode)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We DO NOT:
  • Collect location data (GPS or precise location)
  • Access your contacts, photos, or media library
  • Record audio or video without your explicit action (Text-to-Speech and Speech-to-Text only activate when you use those features)
  • Track your browsing activity outside the App
  • Sell your personal data to third parties

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Services: Enable account creation, authentication, and personalized learning experience
  • Track Learning Progress: Store your vocabulary, test results, streaks, badges, and XP
  • Personalize Content: Recommend words, tests, and practice modes based on your proficiency level and learning goals
  • Sync Across Devices: Backup your progress to Firebase Cloud Firestore so you can access it from multiple devices
  • Leaderboards & Competition: Display your rank on global leaderboards (only displayName and XP are visible to others)
  • Send Notifications: Remind you of daily goals and streak milestones (only if you opt-in)
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • Ensure Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity, unauthorized access
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries sent via email
  • Subscription Management: Process premium subscription payments via RevenueCat

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we share limited information with the following service providers:

3.1 Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google):
    • Firebase Authentication: Manages user sign-up, login, and Google Sign-In
    • Cloud Firestore: Stores user progress, learned words, achievements, and settings
    • Firebase Storage: Hosts audio files (word pronunciations) and images (word illustrations)
    • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports to improve app stability
    • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): Sends push notifications (with your consent)

    Data Shared: User ID, email, displayName, learning progress, device info
    Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

  • Google Sign-In:
    • Enables quick account creation using your Google account
    • We only receive your email, name, and profile photo (no access to other Google services)

    Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

  • RevenueCat:
    • Manages premium subscription purchases (in-app purchases)
    • Processes payment transactions securely (we never see your credit card details)

    Data Shared: User ID, subscription status, purchase receipts
    Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

  • Google Fonts:
    • Provides typography for the App (no personal data collected)
  • Flutter Text-to-Speech (TTS):
    • Converts text to speech for word pronunciation (processed locally on your device, no data sent to servers)
  • Speech-to-Text:
    • Converts your speech to text for pronunciation practice (processed locally, no recordings stored)
  • OpenAI & Gemini (AI-Powered Learning):
    • Generates dynamic stories and dialogues for "AI Roleplay" and "AI Story Fill" learning modes
    • Uses state-of-the-art language models from OpenAI (GPT-4o) and Google (Gemini 1.5 Pro)
    • Optimized for educational context, grammar correction, and natural conversation
    • Automatically falls back to local scenarios if API services are unavailable

    Data Shared: Selected vocabulary words (English, translation, category) — no personal user data
    Privacy Policies: openai.com/privacy-policy, policies.google.com/privacy

⚠️ AI Service Clarification

OpenAI and Gemini AI are used in "AI Roleplay" and "AI Story Fill" modes to generate personalized learning content. The AI receives only vocabulary words (not user progress, scores, or personal data). Generated content is:

  • Designed for educational purposes
  • May contain occasional errors or inaccuracies (typical of AI-generated text)
  • Not stored permanently — generated on-demand and discarded after the session
  • Subject to OpenAI's and Google's content moderation policies

Fallback System: If AI services are unavailable or return errors, the app automatically uses pre-written local scenarios (no difference in user experience).

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL/TLS protocols
  • Cloud Storage: Your learning progress is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore (Google Cloud Platform), which uses industry-standard security measures
  • Local Storage: App settings and temporary data are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences (encrypted by the operating system)
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed using Firebase Authentication's secure algorithms (never stored in plain text)
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access backend systems, and all access is logged

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days (except where legally required to retain)
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for 3+ years may be deleted after email notification
  • Crash Logs: Stored for 90 days for debugging purposes

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in the App settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data (via Settings → About → Delete Account or email us)
  • Export: Download your learning progress as a JSON file (contact us at support@wordmasterapps.com)
  • Opt-Out of Notifications: Disable push notifications in Settings → App Settings
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke Google Sign-In access or delete your account

GDPR Users (EU/EEA): You have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

WordMaster is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at support@wordmasterapps.com, and we will delete it.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on Google Cloud Platform servers, which may be located in the United States or other countries. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations. We ensure that all transfers comply with applicable data protection laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

WordMaster is a mobile app and does not use cookies. However, third-party services like Firebase may use similar technologies (e.g., device identifiers) for analytics and crash reporting. You can limit tracking by disabling analytics in your device settings.

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
