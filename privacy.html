Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to WordMaster. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile picture
  • Learning Progress: Words learned, test scores, daily goals, and study statistics
  • User Preferences: Language settings, notification preferences, and app customization options

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, crash reports, and performance analytics
  • Learning Analytics: Word difficulty ratings, test completion rates, and learning patterns

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core services and track progress
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Improve our app and develop new features
  • Send notifications (only if you opt-in)
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: Authentication, data storage, crash reporting
  • Google Sign-In: Easy account creation
  • AI Services (OpenAI/Gemini): Generate personalized content
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management

Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Update your information
  • Delete your account
  • Opt-out of notifications
  • Export your data

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS, SSL/TLS) to protect your data during transmission. Your learning progress is securely stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore.
